A little about me

I was raised in Florida and have spent most of my life here, but was originally born in Poland -- Polish was actually my first language.

During the day I work in Supply Chain, but I moonlight as a freelance dashboard designer, an amateur-ish programmer, mechanical keyboard enthusiast, and sometimes painter/sewer/general crafty person.

When I get bored of all that (rare) I spend time self-teaching Japanese, reading fantasy novels, playing Fallout 4, or trying my hand at artisan keycap and keyset design. I hope to invest in and experiment with materials in order to open my own shop sometime this coming year!
