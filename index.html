~ A little about me ~

Born in Poland, raised in Florida -- Polish was actually my first language.

During the day I work in Supply Chain, but I moonlight as a programming hobbyist, mechanical keyboard enthusiast, and sometimes painter/sewer.

When I find myself with spare time on my hands (rare) I enjoy self-teaching Japanese, reading fantasy novels, playing Fallout 4, or trying my hand at artisan keycap and key set design. I hope to invest in and experiment with materials in order to open my own shop sometime this coming year!

Catch me on social media to stay up-to-date on keyboard builds, which I'll be streaming on Twitch.
